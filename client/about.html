Our Story

Welcome to CraftedTreasures – a place where creativity meets passion. Born from a love for handmade art and a desire to spread joy through unique, meaningful gifts, we started our journey in a small studio, turning ideas into beautiful, handcrafted creations.

Each product is made with love and care, aiming to add a personal touch to every celebration – be it birthdays, anniversaries, or just everyday moments. Whether you're looking for custom cards, photo frames, or festive decor, our creations are crafted to inspire smiles and memories.

As a small business, every order means the world to us. Thank you for supporting our dream and helping handmade art flourish in a world of mass production.

Our Mission

To spread happiness through handmade crafts that speak from the heart and support local artisans.

Our Vision

To become a trusted destination for unique, heartfelt handmade gifts across India and beyond.

Our Promise

Quality craftsmanship, personalized touch, and 100% customer happiness with every order.
